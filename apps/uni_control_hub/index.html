FAQ.

How does it work?
UniControlHub uses your Desktop platform as an HID device for your Mobile devices, and share Mouse, Keyboard like an actual Bluetooth/Usb devices
Is it available on official stores?
Not yet, its still in development phase, and can be downloaded directly from github repository
Is it free to use?
Yes! UniControlHub is a completely free and open-source tool.
Does it requires any app on Android or iOS?
No, Nothing needs to be installed on Mobile devices, just connect your Mobile with Desktop app and control
How hard is it to setup?
Quite easy. There is a detailed guide with each option in the App, just tap on `i` icon
